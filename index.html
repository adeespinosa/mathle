Mathle

How to Play

Goal

Guess the correct 4-digit number in 12 tries!

Hints

Once you complete a guess, hints will be given on the side.


Positive means that some digits are correct and in right places.

Negative means that some digits are correct but in wrong places.


For example:

Correct answer: 1 2 3 4

Your answer: 1 3 4 2

Hint: -3, +1 --> 3 digits are correct but in wrong places (2,3,4). 1 digit is correct and in right place (1).

Rules

1 - Digits can't repeat!

2 - You only have 12 tries to guess the 4-digit number.

3 - Right-click on the digits to mark them.

4 - Press CLEAR to clear your inputs. Note that once you give your 4th digit, it automatically checks the answer!
